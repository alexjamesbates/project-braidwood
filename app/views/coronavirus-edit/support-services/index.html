Local support services

The coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak means we’re not providing face-to-face support in person right now, but we’re still here to support you over the phone, through video calls or by email.

Our local services can give you information and support to help you:

  • manage your hearing aids during the outbreak
  • manage tinnitus or hearing loss, including the challenges that staying at home brings
  • find useful products for hearing loss or tinnitus
  • help to find or stay in work (Scotland, Northern Ireland and Wales only)
  • find other services and organisations that can support you during this time.
