Wi-Fi doorbells

A Wi-Fi doorbell.



A Wi-Fi doorbell is a type of doorbell that tells you someone is at the door by sending a notification to your mobile.

They work by connecting to the internet through your home Wi-Fi. You set them up and control them through an app on your smartphone or tablet.

They often have more features than traditional and wireless doorbells and door chimes. They can include:

  • A camera built into the device that allows you to see who is at the door on your smartphone or tablet
  • An intercom feature to talk to the person at the door
  • A motion sensor to let you know if someone is at the door even if they haven't rung the bell
  • Separate Wi-Fi connected chimes that also alert you with a chime sound. You usually have to buy these as an optional extra

If you're using a device that connects to the internet like a Wi-Fi doorbell, make sure you know how to protect your personal details. You can find tips for staying safe online from the National Cyber Security Centre.
