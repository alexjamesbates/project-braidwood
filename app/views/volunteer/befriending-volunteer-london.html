Befriending volunteer

Provide conversation and companionship to a deaf person, on a regular basis over time.

Where
Clapham
When
1 day a week, Monday to Sunday
You need to be 16 or over for this role.
Apply

What you'll be doing

As a Befriender you'll:

  • spend time on a regular basis with older deaf and hard of hearing people
  • provide friendship and support
  • go on shopping trips and visits
  • help in a 1:1 setting or group activity
  • train residential home staff in befriending
  • set goals for the year ahead

What you need

It would be good if you:

  • are sensitive towards other people
  • open minded
  • empathetic
  • non-judgemental
  • have a positive attitude
  • reliable, trustworthy and honest
  • have your own transport
  • are committed to the wellbeing of others

What you'll get in return

As a volunteer you'll:

  • learn new skills
  • meet people in different situations
  • make a real difference to the people you support
  • raise awareness in the community
  • get expenses reimbursed
  • try other roles if available
