Adopt a tin volunteer

Help us raise funds for vital services with collection tins in your area.

Where
Around your local area
When
Weekdays or weekends depending on your availability
You need to be 16 or over for this role.
Apply

What you'll be doing

As an Adopt a tin volunteer you will:

  • be an ambassador for the charity in your local area
  • approach businesses by to ‘adopt a tin’ by displaying a collection tin
  • deliver tins to businesses
  • collect full tins and return them to our head office
  • replace full tins with an empty one
  • keep accurate records of your collection tins

What you need

It would be good if you:

  • have good communication and interpersonal skills
  • can motivate people to support Action on Hearing Loss
  • have knowledge of your local area
  • have a network of contacts in your area

What you'll get in return

As a volunteer you'll:

  • help fund vital services that make a real difference to peoples’ lives
  • get regular one-to-one support from our fundraising manager
  • receive training and learn new skills
  • meet people from different backgrounds
  • raise awareness of hearing loss in the community
  • have the opportunity to try other volunteering roles within Action on Hearing Loss
