Coffee morning volunteer

Provide support and friendship to people affected by hearing loss or tinnitus at our Hear to Meet coffee mornings.

Where
Highbhury
When
2 days a month, depending on your availability
You need to be 16 or over for this role.
Apply

What you'll be doing

As a Hear to meet volunteer you will

  • help staff set up the venue before our coffee morning
  • welcome group members when they arrive
  • serve tea and coffee to people throughout the coffee morning
  • listen and chat with group members, their friends and family
  • share your own experiences of deafness, tinnitus or hearing loss
  • promote the Hear to meet group in the area
  • promote the work of the sensory service
  • assist with paperwork when necessary

What you need

It would be good if you:

  • have knowledge of, or are willing to learn about, issues affecting Deaf people, or people who have tinnitus or hearing loss
  • are able to communicate clearly
  • have an interest in working for charities
  • are friendly and approachable
  • are sensitive towards other people
  • are reliable, trustworthy and honest
  • are open-minded and non-judgemental

What you'll get in return

As a volunteer you'll:

  • make a real difference to the people you support
  • help us run this important service
  • receive training and learn new skills
  • meet people in different situations
  • raise awareness of deafness in the community
  • get travel expenses reimbursed
