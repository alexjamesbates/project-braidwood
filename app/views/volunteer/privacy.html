How we will use your personal information

Under the General Data Protection Regulation 2018 and Data Protection Act 2018, we will:

  • only share your information to people with a legitimate reason to receive it
  • never sell it to others
  • send you details of how we store your information if your application is successful
  • keep it for 6 months and then delete it if you're unsuccessful

You can withdraw your consent to our use of your information at any time by emailing volunteering@hearingloss.org.uk.

For further information on how we will protect your personal information and your rights, please read our privacy policy.
