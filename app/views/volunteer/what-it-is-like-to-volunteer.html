What it's like to volunteer at Action on Hearing Loss

Our incredible volunteers make a huge difference to the lives of Deaf people, or people who have hearing loss or tinnitus.

Karl’s volunteering story: "It’s really wonderful"

Karl and his wife started volunteering after they both experienced hearing loss. Together, they now run a hearing aid clinic in Essex.

Karl says:

"I highly recommend getting involved in Action on Hearing Loss. It gives me lots and lots of confidence about my hearing loss and it’s very fulfilling."
