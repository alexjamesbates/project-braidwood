Get help with hearing aids

If you use NHS hearing aids and need new batteries, tubing or minor adjustments, come to a free drop-in service or get a home visit.

Action on Hearing Loss runs this hearing aid support service in partnership with local NHS audiology departments. It is for people who use NHS hearing aids only.

If you need help with hearing aids you bought privately, see your hearing care provider.

How we can help

We can:

  • supply and fit new hearing aid batteries
  • replace hearing aid tubing
  • clean earmoulds
  • help with other minor hearing aid repairs
  • give advice on how to use and clean hearing aids
  • give information on hearing loss and the services and equipment that can help
  • visit you at home if you're unable to travel to a community service

What we can't help with

Unfortunately we can't:

  • provide hearing tests
  • replace or retune hearing aids
  • adjust earmoulds
Contact your audiology clinic if you need these services.
