Local support services

Action on Hearing Loss provides information and support in communities across the UK. Many of our services are run by volunteers who are Deaf or have hearing loss or tinnitus themselves.

We have services to:

  • help you take care of your hearing aids
  • give you information about hearing loss or tinnitus and how to manage it
  • help you find useful products for hearing loss or tinnitus.

We also provide other services in local communities.
