Information and support

Products and technology to help you stay independent and continue to enjoy doing the things you want to do.

Benefits

Baby monitors, multi-alterting systems, smoke alarms, and vibrating pads.

Deaf awareness

Bluetooth streamers, FM systems, loop systems, remote and table microphones, and speech to text software.

Ear health

Extension ringers, wireless door chimes, and Wi-Fi doorbells.


Hearing loss

Amplified telephones, call indicators, and telephone amplifiers.

Products and technology

Apps, handset amplification, HD voice, MFi technology, and talking keypads.

Tinnitus

Relaxation DVDs and CDs, sound relaxers, and tinnitus apps and software.


Work

Remote microphones, subtitles, TV listeners and streamers, and wireless headphones.

Get help

Chat to us online

Whether you need information for yourself or about someone else, our team is online and ready to help.

Call our information line

Ask our dedicated and professional information line officers about any aspect of deafness, hearing loss or tinnitus.

Find a local service

Our services have been supporting people with hearing loss to live the life they choose since 1929.
