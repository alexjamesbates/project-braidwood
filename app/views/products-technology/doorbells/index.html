Overview

A doorbell near the front door or in the hallway may be difficult to hear in other parts of your home, even if you only have mild hearing loss.

If you have a mains-powered doorbell, a qualified electrician may be able to add an extension bell or ringer to it, so you can hear it from elsewhere in your house.

If not a wireless door chime or doorbell might help.
