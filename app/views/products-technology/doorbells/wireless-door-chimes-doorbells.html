Wireless door chimes and doorbells

A wireless door chime or doorbell is a type of doorbell that comes in two parts:

  • A transmitter that acts like a traditional doorbell. You fix it to a front door or porch and it has a button that someone will push when they use it. It is normally battery-operated and weather-proof
  • A device that acts as a receiver that chimes or plays a melody when someone pushes the doorbell. You can then place it anywhere in the house to allow you to hear it better. You can power it by batteries or by plugging it into a mains socket.

  • volume control
  • a selection of sounds and musical melodies to choose from, as some may be easier for you to hear than others
