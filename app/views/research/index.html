Our research into hearing loss and tinnitus

There is currently no cure for hearing loss or tinnitus. We want a future where there are effective medical devices, treatments and cures for those who need it - and we need your help to fund the research to achieve this.

What we fund

Find out more about our three key areas of research and how you can help to support us.

Preventing hearing loss

We want to identify the causes of hearing loss and improve our understanding of it.

Restoring hearing

We want to find ways to restore hearing in the ear and to improve medical devices to help people hear better.

Silencing tinnitus

We want to identify the causes of tinnitus and develop strategies to manage it.
